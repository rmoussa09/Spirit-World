Earth Bending


The Earth Kingdom is the largest and most populated capital of the four great nations. Earthbenders use the balance of strength and defense to overwhelm opponenets. Some of the abilites include:

  • Using earth manipulation to your creativity
  • Using earth manipulation to create shields around your body
  • Using earth manipulation to hear vibrations in the rock beneath your feet
  • And much more!

Fun fact: Select few of the masters of the element can access and advanced techniques called Metalbending and even Lavabending. Metalbending is the ability to manipulate the metal in Earth while Lavabending is the ability to manipulate molten earth.
