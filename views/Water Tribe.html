Water Tribe

Zone A: North of Entrance

Attractions

Following through the first zone of the world, the Water Tribe is an unbelieveable attraction that showcases a zoo with many animals from the ocean! You will also be able to see first hand how the buildings are made through the Ice Slide Coaster and much more!. Passing out snow cones and katara t-shirts, the Water Tribe is not a place to miss.

Water Bending


Only a small amount of the members from the Water Tribe are able to use water bending as their main source of power. Some of the abilites include:

  • Using water manipulation to your creativity
  • Using water manipulation to create ice and increase your versatility
  • Using water manipulation as healing abilites
  • And much more!

Fun fact: Select few of the masters of the element can access and advanced technique called bloodbending. Bloodbending is the ability to manipulate blood in lviing creatures.
