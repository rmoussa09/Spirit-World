Attractions

Following through the third zone of the world, the Fire Nation is where everyone comes together to enjoy the party. From fire dancers walking around the streets and food stands with flaming foods, the culutre is why the Fire Nation is hot. With the addition of the newest attraction, Flaming Rings of Death coaster, tourists can have a great time anywhere in this nation as well as much more!

Fire Bending


The Fire Nation is at the center of the world when it comes to wars. Firebenders use the power of their own energy to draw power. Some of the abilites include:

  • Using fire manipulation to your creativity
  • Using fire manipulation to fly by releasing fire through your feet
  • Using fire manipulation to create heat in cold climates
  • And much more!

Fun fact: Select few of the masters of the element can access anadvanced technique called Lightningbending. Lightningbending is the ability to produce lightning by directing it throught your fingertips. The benders can also redirect the lightning throught their body to another target.
