Attractions

Following through the last and final zone of the world, the Air Nomads is mainly historic as you follow through the journey of air temples. Learn through the history on the Air Nomads Tour while enjoying custom-made glidres for yourself. From temple to temple, you will see life from a different point-of-view and much more!

Air Bending


The Air Nomads is are near extinct and is a rebuilding nation as a whole. Air Nomads mainly use the powers of airbending for defensive purposes only. Some of the abilites include:

  • Using air manipulation to your creativity
  • Using air manipulation to create funnel air vortexs
  • Using air manipulation gain more glide throgh gravity
  • And much more!

Fun fact: Only one master of the element Zaheer can access an advanced technique called Flight. By using Flight, Zaheer was able to seperate himself from the world and be able to fly around as if he was weightless.
